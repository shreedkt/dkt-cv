Biography

I am an educator and software developer who has spent the last years moving between two disciplines that most people treat as separate — and noticing they are not.

I hold an M.C.A from Marwari College, Ranchi University (79.38%), after a B.Sc in Computer Application Honours from Nilamber Pitamber University, Palamu. Between studies and teaching, I worked briefly as a Junior Software Developer at Balaji Amrut Builders & Promoters Pvt. Ltd., Ranchi — shipping PHP/MySQL modules and handling the less-glamorous side of technical ops.

Since March 2024, I have been an Assistant Professor in the B.C.A Department at RVS College of Engineering and Technology, Jamshedpur. Alongside teaching Data Structures with C, Problem Solving in C, and Python Programming, I serve as ERP Co-coordinator, Principal Investigator (PI) of the Helix Tech & AI Club, and Placement Co-ordinator, Department of BCA.

Parallel to teaching, I continue to build. I am the author of the book Blockchain Unlocked, and have co-authored peer-reviewed research on machine learning in healthcare (UGC CARE Group I) and augmented reality in learning environments. My GitHub is where side-projects like a Facial Recognition Login System and a Fog-based Healthcare System live.
