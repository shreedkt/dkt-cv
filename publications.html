Research direction

What I'm curious about next.

My research interests continue to cluster at the intersection of applied machine learning, computer vision, and educational technology — particularly how these fields can serve under-resourced classrooms and healthcare settings.

I am also increasingly interested in blockchain for academic credentialing, fog / edge computing for latency-sensitive healthcare data, and how symbolic knowledge-representation techniques can complement modern deep learning.

Open to co-authorship, thesis supervision collaboration, and cross-institutional research. Let's talk.
